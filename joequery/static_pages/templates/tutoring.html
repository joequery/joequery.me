Why JoeQuery for a tutor? -------------------------

Only 100% comprehension is acceptable

Many professors and textbooks alike have a tendency to gloss over important concepts with a matter-of-factly, "[this is too obvious to even explain][0]" attitude that leaves students discouraged and unprepared for tests. My tutoring sessions thoroughly explore the concepts until all uncertainty is eliminated. [0]: /math/thoughts-on-proof-strategies/

Examples, examples, examples

Higher level concepts, such as recursion and isomorphisms, cannot be understood or applied without a thorough number of examples. My tutoring sessions use examples found from internet resources, multiple textbooks, and real world applications in order to expose the student to different perspectives. Most importantly, I craft my own examples on a per-student basis to exercise specific areas they may not completely understand. After my sessions, not only will you understand the concepts, you can *do something with them*.

I do this because I want to, not because I have to

While some professors at the university level genuinely love to teach, many unfortunately view teaching as an annoyance necessary to continue pursuing their research. I, on the other hand, have a well paying job outside of academia. Thus, tutoring for me is not a requirement. It is instead an opportunity to share my love and enthusiasm for mathematics, computer science, and the acquisition of knowledge. I cannot express how happy I feel when a student tells me they finally "get it" thanks to me.

All presentational materials provided after the session

After our session has concluded, you will receive all material covered in a digital format for your convenience.
